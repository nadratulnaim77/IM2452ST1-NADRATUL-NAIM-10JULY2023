Nurturing Hope, Love, and Bright Futures

Welcome to Angel's Children's Home

The power of love, education, and community.

At Angel's Children's Home, our mission is to provide a safe and loving environment for orphaned, abandoned, and vulnerable children. Established with a vision of nurturing hope and empowering young lives, we are dedicated to offering a brighter future for every child in need.

Activities

World Ice Skating Day 2022 in Sunway Pyramid

Angels Children's Home joined Sunway Pyramid Ice for a free ice skating session on World Ice Skating Day. It was an incredible experience shared with children from different backgrounds. Thank you to Sunway Pyramid Ice and the Ice Skating Association of Malaysia for making this possible.

Magical Movie Night

Angel's Children's Home recently had a magical movie night, filled with laughter and joy. The children gathered together, enjoyed snacks, and watched a wonderful film. It was a heartwarming evening of togetherness and happiness.

World Ice Skating Day 2022 in Sunway Pyramid

Angels Children's Home joined Sunway Pyramid Ice for a free ice skating session on World Ice Skating Day. It was an incredible experience shared with children from different backgrounds. Thank you to Sunway Pyramid Ice and the Ice Skating Association of Malaysia for making this possible.

Magical Movie Night
